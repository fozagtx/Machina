# Domain Expertise Rules

## Web Builds — Context Profile (MANDATORY)

Before writing a single line of HTML/CSS, define:

1. **Brand Identity** — name, personality, color palette, typography tier
2. **ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)** — who they are, pain points, desires, objections
3. **Copy Strategy** — headline formula, value prop, CTA, social proof angle

### Landing Page Copy Rules (Direct Response)
- Headline: outcome + specificity (numbers, timeframe)
- Sub-headline: calls out the ICP pain
- Body: PAS (Problem → Agitate → Solution)
- CTA: action verb + benefit ("Get Your Free Plan" not "Submit")
- Social proof: specific metrics, not vague claims
- No corporate speak, no passive voice

## VSL Scripts

Framework: Perfect Webinar / PAS hybrid

Structure:
1. **Hook** (0–30s) — bold claim or provocative question
2. **Story** (30s–2m) — relatable struggle → turning point
3. **Content** (2m–12m) — 3 secrets / mechanisms (each with proof)
4. **Offer Stack** (12m–16m) — what they get + value justification
5. **Close** (16m–20m) — price reveal, guarantee, urgency, CTA

ElevenLabs formatting:
- `[PAUSE 1s]` for dramatic beats
- `[EMPHASIS]` for key phrases
- Scene markers: `// SCENE: talking head`, `// SCENE: B-roll product`

## UGC Model Scripts

Three frameworks:

**Hook Types:**
- Problem hook: "If you struggle with X, this is for you"
- Curiosity hook: "I tried X for 30 days and here's what happened"
- Result hook: "How I got [specific result] in [timeframe]"

**Body:**
- Personal story (relatable, specific)
- Product mechanism (how it works, not just what it is)
- Proof (before/after, numbers, feelings)

**CTA:**
- Soft: "Link in bio if you want to try it"
- Direct: "Use code [BRAND] for 15% off"
- FOMO: "They're almost sold out — grab yours now"

**B-roll notes:** include shot suggestions in `[brackets]` throughout

## Tweets / Content

Style: direct, opinionated, no hedging, no emoji spam

Formats:
- **Hook tweet**: bold claim in ≤ 12 words
- **Thread**: hook → 5–7 insight tweets → CTA tweet
- **Engagement bait** (ethical): genuine question tied to insight
- **Story tweet**: 1 concrete moment → universal lesson

Conversion principles applied:
- Specificity > generality ("3x revenue" beats "more revenue")
- Contrast creates tension ("Everyone does X. I do Y.")
- End on action or open loop

## Code / Algorithms

- Error handling: only at real boundaries — no defensive noise
